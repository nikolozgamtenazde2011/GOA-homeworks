this page is about parrots

Before buying a parrot, it’s important to learn about their needs and responsibilities. Parrots live a long time and need love, attention, and daily care. Always choose to buy or adopt from a trusted and caring breeder or rescue center. Make sure your parrot has enough space, toys, and a healthy diet to live a happy life.
